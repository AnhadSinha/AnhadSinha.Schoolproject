What do we work for:

We are working to raise a fund to help missions to the moon and the NOVA foundation would be more than pleased to donate to our cause. What we have achieved:

We won the Guinness World Record of the largest donation to a company by 700 million dollars to fund the apollo 14 and 15 missions. We also recieve money from NASA and SpaceX from funding their missions.
